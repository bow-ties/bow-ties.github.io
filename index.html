Background

I'm an artist combining nuanced views of artistic and scientific practices to explore new pedagogical presentations of knowledge production.

In 2021, I graduated from MIT with an SB in Physics and a minor in Art, Culture, and Technology.
While at MIT, I worked on running a multi-purpose STEAM makerspace known as the MIT Edgerton Center Student Project Lab.
Previously, I was the class of 2017 valedictorian of Richland High School in suburban Johnstown, PA.
